Beth Salvatore

I'm from Minnesota but now live in Lexington.

I love rescuing animals, knitting, reading, going to punk shows and playing bagpipes.

My West Coast friends gave me the nickname of "Demoncowgirl" because I moved back to North Dakota, from Los Angeles, and, as a punk rocker, I mostly wore black. That, and they really believed there was nothing in ND except buffalo, Native Americans and outhouses! They also called my "Buffalo Soldier".
